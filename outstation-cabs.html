Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our outstation cab services

In a one-way trip, we drop you at your destination, and the cab returns empty. You only pay for the one-way journey. In a round trip, the cab stays with you throughout your trip and brings you back to your starting point. Round trips are usually more economical if you plan to return within a few days.

For outstation trips, the fare is calculated based on the distance covered (per kilometer charge), driver allowance (for overnight stays), and toll/parking charges. Different vehicle categories have different per-kilometer rates. We provide a detailed fare breakup before confirming your booking.

Yes, you can book a cab for multiple destinations as part of your outstation journey. Simply inform us about your complete itinerary while booking, and we will provide you with a customized package accordingly. This is particularly useful for tourists who want to visit multiple cities in one trip.

If you need to extend your trip, simply inform your driver or contact our customer support. We will adjust your billing accordingly based on the additional time and distance. It's advisable to inform us as early as possible to ensure the driver can accommodate the extended schedule.

Yes, for round trips, there is usually a limit of 250-300 kilometers per day, depending on the package. If you exceed this limit, additional charges apply per extra kilometer. For one-way trips, the charges are based on the actual distance between the source and destination.
